Automated Booking System for a Growing Haircare Brand

Saving 10+ hours a week and eliminating missed appointments — permanently

The Challenge

A growing haircare brand was losing time and revenue to manual booking. Clients were confused about service durations, the team was overwhelmed by rescheduling requests, and missed appointments were hurting the bottom line.

What I Did
  • Configured Acuity Scheduling as a 24/7 automated receptionist — appointment types, availability rules, and buffer times between high-intensity treatments

  • Set up personalised SMS and email reminder sequences with after-care instructions, significantly reducing no-shows
✦ Outcome
100% automated scheduling — zero manual booking calls. 25% fewer no-shows. 10+ admin hours saved every week. Revenue protected through mandatory deposits. Professional, frictionless booking from day one.

My Approach

How I Work With
Founders & Teams

My goal in week one is always the same: create clarity, visibility, and momentum — without creating extra work for you.

Day-to-Day Structure
Daily Check-Ins That Actually Work

15–20 minute daily check-ins structured around three things: top priorities for the day, active blockers, and clear next steps with deadlines. Concise, action-oriented. No unnecessary meetings, no wasted time.

Task Board Design
Every Task Has an Owner & a Deadline

Using Asana, Trello, or Monday, I structure work into Backlog → This Week → Today → Waiting On → Done. Every task has a defined outcome, assigned owner, and specific deadline. Ambiguity removed. Accountability built in from day one.
